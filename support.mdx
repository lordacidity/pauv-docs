## Priority Escalations

Inquiries related to the following are treated as **Priority Escalations** and triaged within 12 business hours:

- Unauthorized account access or suspected fraud
- Account lockouts
- Deposit or withdrawal failures
- Account security concerns

## Reporting Fraud or Unauthorized Access

If you suspect unauthorized access to your account or an unauthorized transaction:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Email support immediately">
    Send an email to **support@pauv.com** with **"URGENT: ACCOUNT SECURITY"** or **"URGENT: FRAUD"** in the subject line.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account freeze">
    Upon receipt, your account and point balance will be **temporarily frozen** to prevent unauthorized activity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit & withdrawal suspension">
    All deposit and withdrawal capabilities for the affected account are suspended during investigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identity verification">
    Our team will verify your identity using our KYC records before proceeding with remediation, refunds, or chargeback investigations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long do withdrawals take?">
    Withdrawals typically take **1–2 business days** for standard ledger reconciliation before funds are remitted to your linked bank account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why was I asked for additional verification?">
    If our risk engine detects elevated risk signals, or if you're a public figure claiming your NPSI profile to direct your good-faith royalty, you may be asked to complete **Step-Up KYC** (government photo ID + selfie liveness check). This protects your account and ensures regulatory compliance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I transfer points or forecasts to someone else?">
    No. Pauv is a closed-loop platform. Peer-to-peer transfers of points, internal tokens, or forecasts are not supported.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if the NPSI moves against my forecast?">
    The NPSI is driven entirely by forecasting activity through DTM 4.0. If forecasting pushes the index against you, closing your forecast will return fewer points than you committed. A Negative Forecast can be automatically liquidated at zero return if the NPSI rises enough to consume 95% of its escrow. Pauv does not offer refunds for NPSI movement or liquidations. See our [Refund Policy](/legal/refund-policy).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I complete KYC verification?">
    KYC is handled through our secure partner Soap Payments during account registration. You'll need to provide your name, date of birth, address, and ID number. See [Getting Started](/getting-started) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm a public figure. How do I claim my NPSI profile?">
    Visit your NPSI profile on pauv.com and select **Claim Your Profile**. You'll complete identity verification (Step-Up KYC) and choose whether to direct your good-faith royalty to a verified bank account or a verified 501(c)(3) charity. See [For Public Figures](/platform/for-talent).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
