## Withdrawals vs. Refunds

Pauv is a **fully liquid, closed-loop platform**. Because you can close your forecasts against the Dual Curve Model at any time and withdraw your credit balance to your linked bank account, Pauv does not offer traditional "refunds" for Platform activity or NPSI-driven losses.

If you wish to retrieve your funds, simply close your active forecasts to receive credits and initiate a withdrawal through the Platform.

## No "Change of Mind" Refunds

We do **not** offer refunds or reversals for:

- Changing your mind after opening a Positive or Negative Forecast
- NPSI movements that result in a loss when you close your forecast
- Fees paid on opening or closing a forecast, including the early-exit guardrail
- Dissatisfaction with Platform features
- Standard withdrawal processing times (1–2 business days)

## Exceptions (Bona Fide Errors)

Pauv Inc. may, at its sole discretion, process a direct refund to your payment method in the following specific scenarios:

| Scenario | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Technical Duplication** | A technical error causes your payment method to be charged twice for a single deposit. |
| **Unauthorized Activity** | We verify that a deposit was made due to fraudulent use of your credit card or bank account (subject to formal investigation). |
| **Failure to Deliver** | You were charged but credits were never delivered to your Pauv account due to a system failure. |

## How to Request a Refund for an Error

To request a refund for a Bona Fide Error, email **support@pauv.com** within **7 days** of the transaction. Include:

1. Your username
2. Transaction ID and date
3. Evidence of the error (e.g., screenshot of a double bank charge)

## Chargebacks

<Warning>
  Initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card issuer **without first contacting Pauv support** constitutes a violation of our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service).
</Warning>

Because credits represent internal ledger balances and forecasts are settled instantly against the Dual Curve Model, unwarranted chargebacks are treated as **friendly fraud**. An unjustified chargeback will result in:

- Immediate and permanent suspension of your Pauv account
- Forfeiture of any open forecasts or credit balance, subject to applicable law
- Potential reporting of the incident to our payment partners
