## 3. Nature of the Platform

Pauv is the world's first real-time index of public sentiment. The Platform hosts the **Net Public Sentiment Index (NPSI)**, a live, dollar-denominated measure of a public figure's cultural relevance, shaped entirely by forecasting activity on the Platform.

- **Not an exchange.** Pauv is not a stock exchange, securities exchange, commodities exchange, prediction market, or a bank.

- **Signal market.** Pauv occupies a category it calls the signal market, distinct from prediction markets (binary settlement) and securities markets (ownership).

## 4. Forecasts and DTM 4.0

- **Forecasts.** A forecast is a directional view on an NPSI expressed through Pauv's proprietary pricing engine, **DTM 4.0**, a single-curve bonding model with a softplus price floor. A **Positive Forecast** (long) pulls the NPSI up; a **Negative Forecast** (escrow-backed short) pulls it down.
- **Negative Forecast liquidation.** Negative Forecasts are fully collateralized by an on-platform escrow equal to twice the user's net stake. A Negative Forecast is automatically closed if the cost to buy back its tokens reaches 95% of its escrow; at that point the user's return is zero. A user cannot lose more than the net stake committed to a Negative Forecast, but may lose all of it in a sharp adverse move of the NPSI.
- **No counterparty matching.** All forecasts settle instantly against DTM 4.0. There is no order book, no bid/ask, no secondary market, and no user-to-user matching.
- **No ownership.** Opening a forecast does not give you ownership of the NPSI, the public figure, or any enterprise. The NPSI is a signal, not an asset.
- **No borrowing; no leverage.** Negative Forecasts do not borrow tokens or funds from other users. Both legs of the escrow originate from the user's net stake and capital extracted from the curve itself. Pauv does not offer margin, lending, or leveraged forecasts.
- **Internal tokens.** DTM 4.0 uses internal token accounting, but users do not directly interact with tokens. Tokens are database records; they are not cryptocurrency, not transferable, and have no value outside the Platform.
- **Algorithmic pricing.** NPSI values are determined solely by forecasting activity through the published DTM 4.0 algorithm. Initial values are set through Elo-Based Comparative Pricing.
