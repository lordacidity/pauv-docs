*Last Updated: April 17, 2026*

Pauv Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share personal information when you visit pauv.com (the "Platform") or use our services. By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect three categories of information from users of the Platform.

### A. Voluntary Data (Information You Provide)

- **Account Registration:** Name, email address, username, and password.
- **Claim Flow (Public Figures):** If you claim an NPSI profile to direct your good-faith royalty, we collect identity verification data sufficient to confirm that you are the individual represented by the profile, plus a verified bank account or the 501(c)(3) designation you choose.
- **Verification Data (KYC):** Government ID numbers, physical addresses, and dates of birth — facilitated securely via Soap Payments.
- **Support:** Information you send when you contact customer support.

### B. Automatic Data (Information Collected via Technology)

- **Device & Usage:** IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and timestamps.
- **Activity:** Pages viewed, time spent on the Platform, and interface interactions.
- **Cookies:** We use cookies and local storage to maintain your login session and ensure platform security.

### C. Transaction Data

- **Financial History:** Records of fiat deposits, credit balances, forecasts opened and closed, good-faith royalty disbursements, and withdrawals.
- **Ledger Records:** Transaction metadata is recorded with tamper-resistant, time-stamped logging for audit purposes.

## 2. Public Figures and Public-Domain Information

NPSI profiles are derived data products reflecting public sentiment about cultural figures. Pauv does not collect, store, or use a public figure's personal information for the purpose of listing their NPSI.

- **No personal data required to list an NPSI.** NPSI profiles exist because public sentiment is public domain.
- **AI-generated imagery.** All visual representations of public figures on the Platform are AI-generated. Pauv does not store or use agency-owned photographs, press images, or biometric data of indexed individuals.
- **Claim-based data collection.** If a public figure chooses to claim their profile, we collect identity verification and payment information at that point, solely to direct the good-faith royalty.

## 3. Third-Party Service Providers

We share necessary data with trusted partners to provide services and comply with financial regulations:

- **Payment & Identity Processing (Soap Payments & Finix):** Pauv does not store your full credit card number or bank account routing details. Financial routing and KYC verification are managed by Soap Payments and Finix.
- **Banking Rails:** Your fiat funds are held in FBO custodial accounts at regulated U.S. depository institutions. Transaction metadata is shared securely via API.
- **Partner Policies:** Financial and identity data will be processed by these partners in accordance with their respective Privacy Policies and federal banking standards.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

- **Service Delivery.** To create and maintain your account, process deposits and withdrawals, settle forecasts, and disburse good-faith royalties.
- **Security & AML Compliance.** To detect coordinated manipulation, structuring, account takeovers, and fulfill Bank Secrecy Act obligations (including OFAC screening, SARs, and CTRs).
- **Communication.** To send transaction confirmations, security alerts, and administrative updates.

## 5. Your Rights (Texas TDPSA Compliance)

Under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA), you have the following rights:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Request a copy of the personal data we maintain about you. |
| **Correction** | Request that we correct inaccuracies in your personal data. |
| **Deletion** | Request that we delete personal data you have provided (financial and KYC records must be retained per AML/BSA law). |
| **Portability** | Request a copy of your data in a digital, machine-readable format. |
| **Appeal** | Appeal our decision if we deny your request. |

**To exercise your rights:** Email **support@pauv.com**. We will authenticate your identity and respond within 45 days.

## 6. Data Security & Retention

- **Security.** We implement industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and bank-grade security protocols for data in transit and at rest.
- **Retention.** Personal information is retained only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Financial and identity records are stored securely for up to 7 years.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Platform is strictly for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 who attempt to open an account. If a Minor's NPSI profile is claimed, only a parent or legal guardian may complete the claim on the Minor's behalf, and only identity verification sufficient to direct the good-faith royalty is collected.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
