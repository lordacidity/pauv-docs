## What Are PVs?

**PVs** are digital collectibles tied to the public sentiment of a Talent — an actor, musician, athlete, creator, or celebrity listed on Pauv.

PVs are similar to digital trading cards. Their value fluctuates based on how many people are buying and selling — a reflection of the public's belief in that Talent's trajectory.

<Warning>
  PVs are **not** equity, stock, cryptocurrency, or financial investments. They are speculative digital collectibles for entertainment and engagement. Do not trade with funds you cannot afford to lose.
</Warning>

## The Linear Pricing Curve

Every Talent's PV uses the same deterministic pricing formula:

- **Starting price:** $0.01 per PV
- **Price increment:** $0.000001 per PV bought or sold

When you **buy** PVs, the price moves up. When you **sell** PVs, the price moves down. This formula is:

- **Identical for all Talents** — No special treatment or customized curves
- **Fully transparent** — The current price and formula are visible to all users
- **Always liquid** — There is always a price at which you can buy or sell

### Example

If a Talent has had 10,000 PVs purchased (net), their current price would be:

$$
\text{Price} = \$0.01 + (10{,}000 \times \$0.000001) = \$0.02
$$

## How to Trade

### Buying PVs

1. Browse Talent profiles on the platform
2. Select the Talent you want to back
3. Enter the amount of USDP you want to spend
4. Confirm your order — it executes instantly against the pricing curve

### Selling PVs

1. Go to your PV portfolio
2. Select the PVs you want to sell
3. Confirm the sale — you receive USDP at the current algorithmic price

All trades are executed **directly against the platform** (Pauv is the counterparty). You are not trading with other users. Shorting is not allowed.

## Transaction Fees

Every buy and sell order incurs a **0.50% total fee**, split evenly:

| Recipient | Share |
|-----------|-------|
| Talent | 0.25% |
| Pauv | 0.25% |

## Important Rules

- **No peer-to-peer transfers** — You cannot send PVs to another user
- **No off-platform trading** — PVs cannot be moved to external wallets or traded on other marketplaces
- **No shorting** — You can only sell PVs you own
- **Price volatility** — If many users sell at once, PV prices can drop significantly

## Indexes

Pauv also offers **Indexes**, which allow you to purchase bundled exposure to multiple Talents at once — similar to thematic baskets. Indexes provide diversification across categories of public figures (e.g., "Rising Athletes" or "Emerging Musicians").
