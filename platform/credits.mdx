## What Are Credits?

**Credits** are Pauv's internal, dollar-denominated ledger balance. You use credits to open forecasts on the platform.

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Exchange rate** | 1 credit = $1 USD (always) |
| **Transferable?** | No — credits cannot be sent to other users, platforms, or wallets |
| **Cryptocurrency?** | No — credits are an internal ledger entry, not a blockchain token |
| **External value?** | None — credits have no value outside of pauv.com |

Think of credits like casino chips: you exchange real dollars for them to use inside the platform, and you can exchange them back at any time.

## Depositing Funds

When you deposit USD into Pauv:

1. Your funds are processed through **Finix** (ACH/card processor) and **Soap Payments** (security gateway).
2. Your USD is deposited into a **regulated For Benefit Of (FBO) custodial account** at a regulated U.S. partner bank.
3. Your Pauv account is credited one-for-one in credits.

<Warning>
  Pauv **never** commingles user funds with corporate operating funds. Your dollars are held separately in regulated custodial accounts at all times.
</Warning>

## Using Credits

All forecasting on Pauv happens in credits:

- **Open a forecast** — Commit credits to the positive or negative curve of a public figure's NPSI.
- **Close a forecast** — Receive credits back at the current curve value.
- **Instant settlement** — Because credits are an internal ledger entry, forecasts open and close instantly without waiting for external payment rails.

## Withdrawing Funds

You can convert your credits back to USD and withdraw at any time:

1. Initiate a withdrawal request through the platform.
2. Pauv performs a standard ledger reconciliation to verify your account history and balance.
3. Funds are remitted to your **linked bank account** within **1–2 business days**.

<Note>
  Withdrawals go back to the same verified bank account you used to deposit. You cannot withdraw to a different account without updating your verification.
</Note>

## Custody & Security

- **FBO accounts** — All user funds are held in For Benefit Of custodial accounts at regulated U.S. depository institutions.
- **No commingling** — User funds are never mixed with Pauv's operational accounts.
- **Encryption** — All financial records are protected with AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest.
- **Audit trail** — Every deposit, forecast, and withdrawal is recorded with tamper-resistant logging.

## Interest on Custodial Funds

Funds held in FBO custodial accounts earn interest while they sit on the platform. Per the [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service), any interest or earnings on pooled custodial balances are the sole property of Pauv Inc. Users have no claim to these earnings. This is a secondary revenue stream that scales with total deposits on the platform.
