## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Visit [pauv.com](https://pauv.com) and create your account with your name, email, and a secure password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your identity (KYC)">
    Before you can deposit funds or open a forecast, you must complete identity verification through our secure partner, Soap Payments.

    **Standard verification includes:**
    - Name, date of birth, and address verification against authoritative databases
    - SMS verification of your mobile device
    - Real-time AML screening against global watchlists

    This process is quick and typically completes in under a minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your bank account">
    Connect your U.S. bank account to enable deposits and withdrawals. Only U.S.-based accounts are supported.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit funds">
    Load funds via ACH or card. Your USD is held in a regulated FBO custodial account, and your Pauv balance is credited one-for-one in internal **credits**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your first forecast">
    Browse NPSI profiles, form a read, and open a **Positive Forecast** (you think cultural relevance will rise) or a **Negative Forecast** (you think it will fall). Every forecast settles instantly against the Dual Curve Model.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Eligibility

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Age** | Must be 18 or older to open a forecast |
| **Residency** | United States only |
| **Verification** | Must pass KYC identity verification |

## Step-Up Verification

In certain cases, you may be asked to complete enhanced verification (Step-Up KYC). This involves uploading a government-issued photo ID and completing a selfie liveness check.

**Step-Up KYC is triggered when:**
- Our risk engine detects device anomalies or elevated risk signals
- Deposit activity triggers enhanced review thresholds
- A public figure is claiming their NPSI profile to direct their good-faith royalty

## Account Security

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account:

1. Email **support@pauv.com** immediately with "URGENT: ACCOUNT SECURITY" in the subject line
2. Your account will be temporarily frozen to prevent unauthorized activity
3. Our team will verify your identity and work with you to secure your account

## Tips for New Users

- **Start small.** Familiarize yourself with the platform and the Dual Curve Model before committing larger amounts.
- **Understand the model.** The NPSI moves based on forecasting activity. Read [How It Works](/how-it-works) to understand the Dual Curve Model and fees.
- **Forecasts are for entertainment.** Opening a forecast is a way to express conviction about public perception. Do not commit funds you cannot afford to lose.
- **Withdrawals take 1–2 business days** for standard ledger reconciliation.
