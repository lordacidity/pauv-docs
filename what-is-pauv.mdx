title: What is Pauv?
description: "The world's first marketplace for human potential."

Pauv is the world's first marketplace for human potential. We let you back the people you believe in by acquiring digital collectibles called **PVs** — tied to the public sentiment of rising talent like athletes, musicians, actors, and creators. Think of it like trading cards, but for real people's cultural trajectory. As public interest in a Talent grows, so does the value of their PVs.
