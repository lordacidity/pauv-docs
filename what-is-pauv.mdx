---
title: What is Pauv?
description: "The world's first real-time index of public sentiment."
---

Pauv is the world's first real-time index of public sentiment. We host the **Net Public Sentiment Index (NPSI)**: a live, dollar-denominated measure of how culturally relevant a public figure is, shaped entirely by forecasting activity on the platform.

Users open **Positive** or **Negative Forecasts** on a public figure's NPSI based on whether they believe that person's cultural relevance will rise or fall. Every forecast settles instantly through Pauv's proprietary **Dual Curve Model**: no counterparty, no binary outcome, no expiration.

Pauv is not a stock exchange, a prediction market, a meme coin, or an NFT project. It is a **signal market**: a continuous, transparent measure of public perception.
