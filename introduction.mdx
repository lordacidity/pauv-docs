## Core Concepts

- **NPSI (Net Public Sentiment Index)**: A live, dollar-denominated measure of a public figure's cultural relevance. Every public figure on Pauv has one. The NPSI is shaped entirely by forecasting activity.
- **Positive Forecast**: A long position on the NPSI, indicating the user believes the figure's cultural relevance will rise. Pulls the NPSI up.
- **Negative Forecast**: An escrow-backed short position on the NPSI, indicating the user believes the figure's cultural relevance will fall. Pulls the NPSI down. Automatically closed if the NPSI rises enough that the buy-back cost reaches 95% of the position's escrow.
- **DTM 4.0**: Pauv's proprietary pricing engine. A single continuous bonding curve with a softplus price floor, driven in opposite directions by Positive and Negative Forecasts. Settles every forecast instantly with no counterparty.
- **Good-faith royalty**: 0.5% of every forecast transaction, set aside for the public figure whose NPSI is being forecasted. Claimable, donatable to a verified 501(c)(3), or escheated to the state.
- **Points**: Internal USD-denominated ledger balance used to open forecasts on Pauv. 1 point = $1 USD. Points have no value outside of Pauv and cannot be transferred to other users.

## Key Features

- **Continuous signal**: The NPSI is live and always on. It has a floor at zero and no ceiling, moving in real time as users open and close forecasts.
- **Instant settlement**: Every forecast is priced and settled instantly by DTM 4.0. No order book, no counterparty matching, no liquidity requirements.
- **Two-sided expression**: Users can forecast that cultural relevance will rise (Positive) or fall (Negative). Both directions drive the same curve in opposite directions, producing a self-correcting signal.
- **Closed-loop platform**: Points and internal tokens cannot leave Pauv. All activity happens inside a single, regulated environment.
- **Bank-grade custody**: User funds are held in For Benefit Of (FBO) custodial accounts at regulated partner banks. Pauv never commingles user funds with operating funds.
- **MSB-registered**: Pauv is a registered Money Services Business with full KYC and AML compliance.

## What Pauv Is Not

Pauv occupies a category it calls the **signal market**, distinct from any other market structure:

| Pauv Is Not | Why |
|-------------|-----|
| A stock exchange or securities market | Forecasts are not ownership, equity, debt, or investment contracts. |
| A prediction market | There are no binary outcomes, no event-based settlement, and no expiration. |
| A meme coin platform or NFT marketplace | There are no transferable tokens and no blockchain assets users can hold. |
| A crowdfunding or creator monetization tool | Users are not funding projects. Forecasts measure perception, not intent. |

Pauv's official industry category is **Technology, Information, and Internet**.
